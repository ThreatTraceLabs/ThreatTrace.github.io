About ThreatTrace

ThreatTrace is my personal cybersecurity research hub where I document my journey in security research and vulnerability discovery. This platform serves as a knowledge base for security professionals, students, and enthusiasts looking to understand modern attack vectors and defensive strategies.

Through this site, I share detailed technical writeups, proof-of-concept exploits, custom security tools, and educational content covering various domains including web application security, network penetration testing, mobile security, and reverse engineering.

Whether you're here to learn about a specific CVE, looking for CTF solutions, or searching for security tools, you'll find thoroughly researched and documented content aimed at advancing the cybersecurity community.
